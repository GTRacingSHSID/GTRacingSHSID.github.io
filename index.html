About Us

SHSID GT Racing continues the legacy of a team with the same name, one of the most prolific teams around the competition. Founded by team leader Zhang Gabriel Jia Xiao in 2017, GT Racing was a club in Shanghai High School International Division (SHSID).

From the graduation of Gabriel Zhang, the team has once lost its advantage around the race, but under the hard work of our team members, who started from nothing, we managed to revive the glory of the team and achieve even higher accomplishments and successes than before.

Our Achievements

Racing Excellence

Founded by team leader Zhang Gabriel Jia Xiao in 2017, GT Racing has grown into one of the most prolific teams in the competition.

We are a three-time NSL China Stop champion (2019, 2023, 2025) and a two-time global third place winner in the Ten80 Racing Challenge (2019, 2024).
