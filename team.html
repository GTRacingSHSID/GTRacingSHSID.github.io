Team

Team Structure

GT Racing is organized into three main departments, each with specialized roles:

Engineering Department

This department takes care of the design, development, manufacturing, and testing of our racing cars. The engineering department handles mechanics, electronics, software, and performance testing.

Business Department

The Business Department handles all non-technical aspects of the team, including sponsorship, marketing, finance, and event management. They ensure the team has the resources needed to compete at the highest level.

Public Relations Department

Our Public Relations Department manages the team's public presence, including media content, brand image, communications, and community engagement. They create compelling content and maintain our team's public image.
